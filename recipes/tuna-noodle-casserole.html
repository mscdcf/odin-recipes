Tuna Noodle Casserole

Description

Tuna tossed with noodles, peas and pimiento in a creamy sauce is baked until bubbling with crunchy crumb topping.

This recipe has been a part of my family for generations! Many happy times took place at the table while partaking in this tasty, yet easy to cook, dinner!

Ingredients

  • 1 (10.75 ounce) can of Campbell's Condensed Cream of Celery Soup
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 cup cooked peas
  • 2 tbsp chooped pimentos (optional)
  • 2 (6 ounce) cans tuna, drained and flaked
  • 2 cups hot cooked medium egg noodles
  • 2 tbsp dry bread crumbs
  • 1 tbsp butter or margarine, melted

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (205 C)
  2. Mix soup, milk, peas, pimiento, tuna and noodles in 1-1/2 quart baking dish
  3. Bake for 20 minutes
  4. Mix bread crumbs with butter. Sprinkle on top. Bake 5 minutes or until hot.
