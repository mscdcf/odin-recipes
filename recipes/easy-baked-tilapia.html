Easy Baked Tilapia

Description

This easy recipe for tilapia only takes a few minutes to prepare, uses few ingredients, and is flavorful.

Farmed fish that grow quickly, have low mercury levels, feed on algae and plant-based foods, and reduce fishing pressure on prey species...what more could you ask for?

Ingredients

  • 4 (4 ounce) fillets tilapia
  • 2 tsp butter
  • 1/4 tsp Old Bay Seasoning, or to taste
  • 1/2 tsp garlic salt, or to taste
  • 1 lemon, sliced
  • 1 (16 ounce) package frozen cauliflower with broccoli and red pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 C). Grease 9x13 inch baking dish
  2. Place the tilapia fillets in the bottom of the baking dish and dot with butter. Season with Old Bay seasoning and garlic salt. Tope each one with a slice or two of lemon. Arrange the frozen mixed vegetables around the fish and season lightly with salt and pepper
  3. Cover the dish and bake for 25-30 minutes in the preheated oven, until vegetables are tender and fish flakes easily with fork
